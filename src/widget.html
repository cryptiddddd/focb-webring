copy-paste snippet

within the <head> element of your page, add the following line:


    <script src="https://friends-of-cranebot.pages.dev/assets/js/remoteWidget.js" type="module"></script>

then, where you would like your widget to go within the <body> of your page, insert a <div> with the id focbwr, like so:


    <div id="focbwr"></div>

and that is IT!!!!!!
